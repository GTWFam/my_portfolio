BIO

My name is Hoang, but english-speaking people call me Bill. My origin lies in Hanoi, Vietnam. Both of my parents are Vietnamese, but in 1994 they decided to move to Russia (that is also the year when my older sister was born). Six years later in a Moscow hospital I was brought to life and started my adventurous life journey. Growing up I was taught Vietnamese by my parents and Russian by the environment. Then I started learning English in school as it was the requirement. As a result, I am a junior at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in the United States where I started pursuing Computer Science major.
